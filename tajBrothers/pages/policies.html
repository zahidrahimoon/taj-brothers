Anti-Bribery, Fraud & Corruption

Taj & Brothers Enterprises maintains a zero-tolerance stance on bribery, fraud, and corruption. We prohibit all forms of bribery and have effective measures in place to prevent, detect, and address these issues.

  • Regular training and clear guidelines
  • Strong reporting channels for unethical behavior
  • Continuous monitoring and audits
  • Commitment to integrity and transparency

Environmental Stewardship Policy

Taj & Brothers Enterprises is committed to environmental responsibility. We actively work to reduce our environmental impact through waste reduction, recycling programs, and energy-efficient practices. We support and implement sustainable solutions and continuously seek ways to improve our environmental performance to contribute positively to the community and the environment.

Quality Assurance

We are dedicated to maintaining high standards of quality in all our products and services. Our quality assurance practices include regular inspections, supplier evaluations, and adherence to established quality control processes. We continuously review and improve our quality standards to ensure customer satisfaction.

Client Confidentiality Policy

At Taj & Brothers Enterprises, we are committed to safeguarding client information through stringent security measures, including secure storage, restricted access, and regular audits, ensuring the highest level of confidentiality and privacy.

Child Labour Policy

We do not employ individuals under the age of 14. For those aged 15 to 18, work hours are scheduled to avoid conflicts with their education. Hazardous work is restricted to individuals aged 18 and older.

Safeguarding, Health, and Safety Policy

Taj & Brothers Enterprises upholds a zero-tolerance stance on abuse, exploitation, bullying, and misuse of power. This policy protects all individuals, regardless of age, gender, sexuality, disability, religion, or ethnicity.

We ensure a safe and healthy work environment by complying with all applicable laws and regulations. Our commitment includes regular safety training, maintaining a secure workplace, and proactive accident prevention.

Ethical Conduct

Taj & Brothers Enterprises requires all employees and partners to adhere to high ethical standards. This involves managing conflicts of interest, avoiding unethical behavior, and ensuring transparency in all business dealings. We provide clear guidelines on expected conduct and have procedures in place for reporting and addressing unethical behavior.

Compliance

Taj & Brothers Enterprises is committed to full compliance with all relevant laws and regulations. We conduct regular compliance checks and provide training to employees to ensure understanding and adherence to legal and regulatory requirements. Any compliance issues are addressed promptly and transparently.

Supplier Code of Conduct

Our Supplier Code of Conduct outlines the standards we expect from our suppliers, including compliance with labor laws, fair trade practices, environmental responsibility, and quality standards. We require our suppliers to adhere to these standards and conduct regular assessments to ensure compliance.

Our Registrations

We are registered with the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR), Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC), and Sindh Revenue Board (SRB), ensuring compliance with all relevant tax and regulatory requirements.

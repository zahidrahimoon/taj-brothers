Who We Are

About Taj & Brothers

Leading General Trading & Construction Company

Taj & Brothers Enterprises, established in 2017 and registered in 2020, is a leading general trading and construction company based in Umerkot and Hyderabad, Sindh. We are registered with PEC and the Sales & Income Tax authorities and are committed to the highest ethical standards, emphasizing fair labor practices and respect for human rights.

Our Expertise Includes:

  • Comprehensive Construction Services, Material Supplies, and Civil Works
  • General Trading
  • Import & Export
  • Strategic Sourcing and Procurement
  • Administrative and Mobilization Services
  • Trade and Technology Solutions

We actively combat modern slavery and human trafficking, ensuring transparency and integrity in our operations. By partnering with NGOs, educational institutions, and the health sector, we drive positive change.

Our Commitments:

  • Adhering to international labor standards
  • Promoting workers' rights and freedom of association
  • Eliminating forced and child labor
  • Advancing environmental sustainability and green technologies
  • Upholding business integrity

Taj & Brothers Enterprises is recognized for its unwavering commitment to ethical practices and client satisfaction, establishing us as a trusted partner in the trading sector.
